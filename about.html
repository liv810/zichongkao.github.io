About Kao

I'm a UChicago undergrad, future data scientist, passionate sailor and Singaporean. You can find me online on Gmail, Quora, Facebook, Github and LinkedIn.

Undergrad

I am about to graduate from the University of Chicago. College has been a buffet of knowledge, and I've stuffed myself silly with many different kinds of food. It started out with physics, then math, economics, statistics, and CS. Some of the highlights were Marx's theory of Capitalism, point set topology and statistical machine learning.

Data Scientist

I'm interested in using data to understand human behavior. Although humans possess a strong ability to empathize, algorithms supplied with sufficient data often outperform us at modelling the behavior of our own species. This fascinates me deeply. After I graduate, I'll be headed to the Bay Area to join Quora as a data scientist.

Sailor

I grew up sailing and enjoy racing immensely. For the past few years, I have been running the UChicago Sailing Club. We race 420s in the Midwest college racing circuit. I've also been doing some keelboat racing: I skippered a J80 at the EDHEC Sailing Cup (below), and was bowman on the St. Francis during the ill-fated 2011 Chicago Mackinac Race.

But racing Lasers is my true calling, and I hope to get back to it soon. I'm also currently seeking a position on the 2015 Transpac. Taking part would be a dream come true.
