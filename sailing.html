Background


2011 - 2014: Ran the University of Chicago Sailing Club. Raced 420s in the Midwest college racing circuit. Skippered a J80 at the 2013 EDHEC Cup, Brest, France.

Bowman on Jairo and Maria De Cruz's St Francis, a 48-foot X482. Did the Chicago Mackinac Race with them in 2011 and 2012. Also raced on Chris Boome's boat with David LaPier in SF over the summer of 2013.
2008: Entered the army. Didn't have the time nor will to sail hard during my time off. Got into keelboat racing. Won the 2008 Horsburgh Challenge.
2006 - 2007: I had the idea that I would try make the national team. I took out a long-term rental on a Laser and trained intensively on my own for about a year. Placed 11th overall and 6th youth in the 2007 Singapore Laser Open.
2003: Bytes were introduced to Singapore. I was outgrowing the Optimist, and decided to switch over.
1999 - 2002: Raced in the Singapore Optimist Fleet. Best ranking: ~40/200+.
1999: Learned to sail on blue wooden Optimists on the Straits of Johor - the waterway separating Singapore from Malaysia. I was ten and my best friend's parents had invited me to join him on the "Get Kids Afloat" course at SAFYC.
